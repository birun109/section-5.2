GIGW 3.0 Statement 5.2.28: Page Titled (WCAG 2.4.2)

Analysis and Requirements

**WCAG Reference****Success Criterion 2.4.2 Page Titled**
**WCAG Level****Level A**
**Clause in Short Form**Pages Must Have Descriptive Titles
**Goal**Ensure all users, especially those using screen readers or browsing with multiple tabs, can quickly understand the purpose of a web page.
**GIGW Requirement**Title must be descriptive. For top-level pages (Homepage), include the **full governmental entity name** and **geographic reference** (e.g., "Government of India").

Example for Success Criteria ✅

Success Example 1: Compliant Homepage Title (GIGW Format)

Scenario: The title for the main entrance page of a ministry.

HTML Title Tag: <title>Ministry of Health & Family Welfare, Government of India</title>

**Compliance Justification:** The title is **descriptive** ("Ministry of Health & Family Welfare") and includes the **geographic/jurisdictional reference** ("Government of India") as specifically required by the GIGW standard for top-level pages. This is the ideal format for government entities.

Success Example 2: Compliant Interior Page Title (Descriptive)

Scenario: The title for a specific form page on a state government website.

HTML Title Tag: <title>Apply for Property Tax Exemption - Dept. of Revenue, Govt. of Karnataka</title>

**Compliance Justification:** The title clearly states the **purpose/topic** of the page ("Apply for Property Tax Exemption") and includes the necessary **website context** ("Dept. of Revenue, Govt. of Karnataka"). This is highly useful for navigation and bookmarks.

Example for Failure Criteria ❌

Failure Example 1: Generic or Default Title

Scenario: A new page created from a template that hasn't been updated.

HTML Title Tag: <title>Untitled Document</title> OR <title>Page 1 of 5</title>

**Compliance Justification:** The title does **not describe the topic or purpose** of the page. This is confusing for screen reader users and users with multiple tabs open, as all pages might have the same meaningless title, failing SC 2.4.2.

Failure Example 2: Vague or Insufficient Title (GIGW Specific)

Scenario: A homepage title that omits necessary GIGW details.

HTML Title Tag: <title>Health Ministry Homepage</title>

**Compliance Justification:** While technically describing the topic, the title is **too vague** and fails the GIGW standard for government entities. It omits the **full name** and **jurisdictional reference** ("Government of India"), making the source of the information unclear to the user and in search results.
