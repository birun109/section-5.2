Shortcut Remapping Example

Scenario

A complex online dashboard originally uses the global shortcut 'C' to open the Client History panel.

User Settings

Users may remap the default shortcut from 'C' to another option requiring a non-printable key:

Compliance Justification

The system provides a mechanism to remap the risky single-character shortcut to one requiring Ctrl or Alt. This makes it harder to trigger accidentally by speech or unintended key presses, aligning with accessibility requirements.
