GIGW 3.0 Statement 5.2.3: Captions (WCAG 1.2.2)

Clause: Pre-recorded Audio Content in Synchronised Media

Goal: Provide users who are deaf or hard of hearing access to the audio content of a video.

Requirement: Synchronized **Captions** must be provided for all pre-recorded videos with audio.

✅ Success Example: Video with Full Captions

Scenario: A pre-recorded video announcement by the Ministry of Education.

Caption Content Example (from `announcement-captions.vtt`)

00:00:01.000 --> 00:00:04.500 **[SOUND: Opening fanfare music]** 00:00:05.100 --> 00:00:09.800 **[Secretary Sharma]:** Good afternoon. We are delighted to confirm the new policy. 00:00:10.200 --> 00:00:13.000 **[Reporter]:** Will this affect the budget allocation?

**Compliance Justification:** Captions are provided, synchronized, and they **include speaker identification** (Secretary Sharma, Reporter) and **non-speech sounds** (**[SOUND: Opening fanfare music]**), ensuring deaf users receive equivalent auditory information.

❌ Failure Example 1: Missing Captions Entirely

Scenario: A video tutorial for filing a tax return, with voice narration.

**Compliance Justification:** The full voice narration containing crucial instructions is inaccessible to users who are deaf or hard of hearing because **no captions are provided**.

❌ Failure Example 2: Incomplete Captions (Missing Sound Effects)

Scenario: A weather safety video where a thunderclap is used as an urgent warning cue.

Incomplete Caption Content Example (Simulated Failure)

00:00:01.000 --> 00:00:04.000 Seek shelter immediately. The storm has arrived.

**Compliance Justification:** If the loud **thunderclap** is the primary signal of immediate danger, and the captions omit **[SOUND: Loud Thunderclap]**, a deaf user misses the urgent, non-speech auditory cue. The captions are not equivalent, resulting in a failure.

Summary of Conformity

GIGW Statement WCAG Reference WCAG Level Required Action (Govt. & Dev.)
5.2.3 (Captions) SC 1.2.2 A Provide synchronized captions for all pre-recorded videos, including **dialogue, speaker identification, and all meaningful sound effects**.
