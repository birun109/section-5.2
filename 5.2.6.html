GIGW 3.0 Statement 5.2.6: Audio Description (WCAG 1.2.5)

Clause: Pre-recorded Video Content in Synchronised Media

Goal: Provide users who are blind or visually impaired access to the **visual information** conveyed by the video.

Requirement: An **Audio Description (AD)** track must be provided for the video.

✅ Success Example: Video with Separate Audio Description Track

Media: A public health announcement video featuring statistics on-screen.

Main Audio Dialogue: "The data clearly shows improvement in three key areas." (The speaker does not read the data.)

Simulated Audio Description Content (The AD Track)

00:00:08.000 --> 00:00:12.000 (AD: A bar chart appears, titled 'Public Health Metrics.' **Cases dropped from 150 to 50** in three months.)

This is compliant because the AD track is provided to fill the informational gap. Users can select this track to hear the description of the visual data.

✅ Success Example (Exception): Audio Content Already Equivalent

Media: A video of a speaker presenting, where the speaker verbally describes everything on their slides.

Main Audio Dialogue: "This slide, which shows our budget breakdown, clearly indicates 40% goes to training, 30% to R&D, and the remaining 30% to operations."

**Compliance Justification:** No separate AD is necessary because the **main audio track already provides all equivalent visual information** (the exact breakdown numbers) to the user. This meets the criteria for the exception.

❌ Failure Example: Video Missing Visual Context

Media: A ceremonial video showing the signing of a major agreement.

Main Audio Dialogue: "We welcome the delegates from the Ministry of Finance." (Silence follows.)

**Visual-Only Event:** During the silence, the video shows the two Ministers shaking hands, sitting down, and **signing the document with a close-up of the names**. Without an Audio Description track (**AD: The Minister and Deputy Minister sign the documents; their names are visible on the contract.**), the visual action and the crucial written text are inaccessible to blind users, resulting in a Level AA failure.

Summary of Conformity

GIGW Statement WCAG Reference WCAG Level Required Action (Govt. & Dev.)
5.2.6 (Audio Description) SC 1.2.5 AA Provide a separate **Audio Description (AD) track** for all pre-recorded videos where visual information is not already present in the main audio.
