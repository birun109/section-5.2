GIGW 3.0 Statement 5.2.2: Audio-only and Video-only (WCAG 1.2.1)

Clause: Alternatives for Pre-recorded Audio/Video-only Content

1. Pre-recorded Audio-only Content

✅ Success Example: Audio-only with Full Transcript

Media: An audio file of a public service announcement (PSA).

Full Transcript (Equivalent Text Alternative)

[Speaker 1]: This is a reminder regarding the deadline for property tax filing. **[SOUND: Clock ticking]**

[Speaker 2]: The new extended deadline is October 31st. Visit our portal for details.

**Compliance Justification:** The full text transcript provides the **equivalent information** from the audio, including dialogue and critical sound effects, making it accessible to deaf users.

❌ Failure Example: Audio-only with No Equivalent Alternative

Media: A recorded welcome message from the department head.

**Compliance Justification:** The message is inaccessible to users who are deaf or cannot play audio, as no **text alternative (transcript)** is provided.

2. Pre-recorded Video-only Content

✅ Success Example: Video-only with Equivalent Text Alternative (Transcript)

Media: A silent instructional video demonstrating a security check (using only visual cues and text overlays).

Full Visual Description (Equivalent Text Alternative)

[Visual]: Screen shows the login page. **Text overlay: Enter OTP.**

[Visual]: The cursor moves to the top menu, then highlights the **'Security Logs' link** in green.

**Compliance Justification:** The text alternative (visual description) provides **equivalent information** for the visuals, making the instructions accessible to users who are blind or cannot see the screen.

❌ Failure Example: Video-only with No Alternative

Media: A silent time-lapse video showing the progress of a key infrastructure project over six months.

**Compliance Justification:** The video contains critical information (visual progress over time, specific construction events) that is inaccessible to users who are blind, as neither an **audio track nor a text transcript/description** is provided.

Summary of Conformity

GIGW Statement WCAG Reference WCAG Level Required Action (Govt. & Dev.)
5.2.2 (Audio/Video-only) SC 1.2.1 A Provide a **transcript** for audio-only content. Provide either a **transcript/description** or an **audio track** for video-only content.
