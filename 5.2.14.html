GIGW 3.0 Statement 5.2.14: Contrast Minimum (WCAG 1.4.3)

Analysis and Requirements

**GIGW Statement**The visual presentation of text and images of text has a contrast ratio of at least 4.5:1...
**WCAG Reference****Success Criterion 1.4.3 Contrast (Minimum)**
**WCAG Level****Level AA**
**Clause in Short Form**Text Contrast Must Be 4.5:1 Minimum
**Goal**To improve readability and accessibility for users with visual impairments or color deficiencies.
**Requirement**Standard text: **4.5:1**. Large text (18pt reg. or 14pt bold): **3:1**. Exemptions: Logos, incidental text, inactive components.

Suitable Success Examples ✅

Success Example 1: Standard Text Contrast (4.5:1 required)

Scenario: Main body text on a white background.

This text meets the required 4.5:1 ratio (approx. 15:1). This ensures that standard body text is highly legible for most users, including those with moderate low vision.

**Compliance Justification:** The text color (`#1A1A1A`) on the background (`#FFFFFF`) easily exceeds the 4.5:1 minimum ratio for standard-sized text, fulfilling the primary requirement of SC 1.4.3.

Success Example 2: Large Text Contrast (3:1 required)

Scenario: A section header that is large and bold.

This Large Text Meets the 3:1 Ratio!

**Compliance Justification:** Since the text is **large (20pt bold)**, it only requires a **3:1 minimum ratio**. The text color (`#A9A9A9`) on the background (`#0000FF`) achieves a ratio of approximately 3.1:1, meeting the less stringent requirement for large-scale text.

Suitable Failure Examples ❌

Failure Example 1: Standard Text Below 4.5:1

Scenario: Small legal disclaimer text using a light gray color on a light gray background.

This text has a contrast ratio of only 1.6:1 (FAIL). This text size requires 4.5:1.

**Compliance Justification:** The text is standard size and requires a 4.5:1 ratio. Since the ratio is only 1.6:1, it fails the criterion. Text with such low contrast is virtually unreadable for users with low vision or older users.

Failure Example 2: Large Text Below 3:1

Scenario: A large, non-bold heading that should meet the 3:1 minimum.

Large Heading with Poor Contrast

**Compliance Justification:** Even though the text is large, its ratio is approximately 1.6:1. Since large text requires a minimum ratio of 3:1, this color combination fails the criterion. The visual prominence of the size is insufficient to overcome the poor contrast.

Summary of Conformity

WCAG Reference WCAG Level Key Action for Compliance
SC 1.4.3 Contrast (Minimum) AA Use a **contrast checking tool** to verify all text (and images of text) colors against their background. Ensure **standard text meets 4.5:1** and **large text (18pt reg. or 14pt bold) meets 3:1**. Do not use low-contrast text unless it falls under an exemption (logo, incidental, decorative).
